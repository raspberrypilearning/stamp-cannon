## What you will make

Build a playful browser toy where a cannon fires colourful stamped shapes in a looping volley that stretches from the barrel to your pointer.

Move the mouse to aim the cannon, click to fire, use the UP and DOWN keys to change how many stamps appear, press 1, 2, or 3 to switch shape, and press C to clear the canvas.
